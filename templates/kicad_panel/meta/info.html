Panel wiring

A starter project for drawing panel / harness wiring at 1:1 scale in the PCB editor, ready for the harness_engine exporter (Tools → External Plugins → Generate harness docs).

  • Net classes = wire types, pre-made with colors and visual widths: 14AWG_BN, 16AWG_BU, 18AWG_RD, 1.5MM2_GNYE, CTRL_GY. Tracks render in the wire color while you route.
  • 600 × 400 mm panel outline on Edge.Cuts — resize to your enclosure. Use User.Drawings for DIN rails and wire duct.
  • harness_specs.yaml starter next to the board, pre-mapped to the classes.

Workflow: schematic → assign nets to wire-type classes → Update PCB → place device footprints at their real panel positions → route each wire at real scale → export. Routed length becomes the wire's cut length.
